About Flyght Zone

I plan to open the first FPV Raceway right here in Chattanooga. It will be a family fun center built around the rush of excitement of first- person-view (FPV) drone racing. I have my sights set on a location right behind Southside Social that is about to break ground on a major renovation. The raceway will feature birthday/group event rooms, a redemption arcade, and a flight academy exhibit. We will also hold summer programs that teach kids to program drones.

An excellent execution of the flight academy is absolutely essential to success as new comers may get frustrated and leave if not properly equipped to handle piloting a drone. I am working on a mobile game that will take absolute beginners through the basics of remote control drone operation, safety, and racing. The flight academy will feature multiple exhibits of my own design that allow a novice to gain hands-on experience remote controlling a drone in a safe, almost indestructible mini-arena.

This is very early stages at the moment, but with the right alignment of mentors, investors, and the community, we can launch the first location in Chattanooga as early as April 2016.
