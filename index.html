Flyght School

Flyght School is absolutely essential to train newcomers to pilot a drone. Before you go to the race pit, you'll have to go through our flight school. This will likely only be needed once, but may require a refresher course if time between visits is long.

Step 1

Video Overview

We'll have an introductory video to get you the basics of remote control drone operation, safety, and racing.

Step 2

Video Game Simulation

Operate a virtual drone as you progress through a series of challenges that will prepare you for the real-world operation of drones.

Step 3

Flyght Exhibit

Gain hands-on experience remote controlling a drone in a safe mini-arena.
